About the Walk

Harajuku Fashion Walk originally started in Tokyo. It’s a colorful and inclusive event where everyone can express their unique style while walking together.

Join our community to show your kawaii style, make new friends, and celebrate Harajuku fashion with us!

Photo by TokyoFashion.com

What's Harajuku?

Harajuku fashion, born in Tokyo’s Harajuku district, is vibrant and diverse. It features bold colors, playful patterns, and a wide variety of unique styles, all celebrating individuality and self-expression.
